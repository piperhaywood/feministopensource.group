Open source investi­gations are about power relations, so too is feminism. Together we asked: what would a feminist open source investi­gation look like?

The Feminist Open Source Investigations Group (FemOS) is an alliance of human rights and tech workers. We have been meeting online and in-person in London and New York since October 2019. During this time we have shared our experi­ences of working on investi­gations and writing code; we have discussed and defined our politics; we have worked to build a digital and social structure for the groupâ€™s activities.
